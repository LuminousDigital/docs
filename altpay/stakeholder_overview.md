## 1. What is altpay?

**altpay is a payment gateway** ‚Äì a technology platform that allows Nigerian businesses to accept payments from their customers online.

A payment gateway built specifically for Nigeria, designed to help businesses accept payments easily and securely.

### In Simple Terms:
> When a customer buys something online from a Nigerian business, altpay handles the payment ‚Äì securely taking money from the customer's card and depositing it into the business's bank account.

### Key Facts:
- üéØ **Target Market**: Nigerian SMEs, startups, and e-commerce businesses
- üí≥ **Payment Methods**: Card payments (Visa, Mastercard, Verve)
- üí∞ **Pricing**: 1.5% per transaction (capped at ‚Ç¶2,000)
- ‚ö° **Settlement**: Next business day
